About Sudip

Sudip Mondal has been suffering from Cervical Dystonia since 2010. In 2023 he underwent Deep Brain Stimulation (DBS) surgery at NIMHANS, Bangalore with the help of friends and donors.

Unfortunately, the implanted DBS device battery is now leaking and failing. Doctors warned that this could cause permanent brain damage and life-threatening complications.

Sudip is currently bedridden and unemployed. His mother, who is also a heart patient, is taking care of him.

Doctors at the Institute of Neurosciences, Kolkata advised urgent surgery to replace the DBS battery.
